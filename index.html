About

Hi there! It is very nice to meet you!

I graduated for the University of Colorado at Boulder in May of 2015. My degree is a Bachelor's of Science in Computer Science. While in school, I was very busy. I lead the Women in Computing Group for 2 years, and worked hard to ensure equality for every technical woman on campus. I also discovered a love for Video Games, when a friend introduced me to the Kingdom Hearts series and the wonder of Steam.

Now, I continue to work hard as an advocate for diversity in Computer Science. I volunteer with the Boulder CoderDojo and the Innovation Center to introduce children of all ages to computers and technology. I have continued my partnership with the Women in Computing Group at CU and have positioned myself as an industry consultant and mentor to the team. I am also working as a Jr. Application Developer at Zayo Group. I take my experience in leadership to bring new insights to the company, and am currently working on a system to assist in developer management within our Salesforce environment.

When I'm not being an advocate or working, I spend my time at the gym, engineering games, reading, writing music, making online videos and blogs, working on story ideas with friends and playing games until my wrists hurt.
